# 📚 SpineVision: User Walkthrough Guide

Welcome to **SpineVision**, the ultimate ecosystem for book resellers. This guide will walk you through your first sourcing trip and show you how to scale your business from a Hobbyist to a full-scale Enterprise using our AI-driven tools.

---

## 🏁 1. Getting Started: The Onboarding
When you first open the SpineVision app:
1.  **Identity Sync:** You will be automatically signed in. Your "Vision Tier" (Hobbyist, Pro, or Enterprise) determines which advanced modules you can access.
2.  **Home Dashboard:** Your main command center showing your current inventory value, active sourcing trips, and "BOLO" (Be On Look Out) alerts.

---

## 🔍 2. Phase One: The Sourcing Trip (OmniVision)
You’re at a thrift store or a library sale. It's time to find gold.

### Step 1: Fast Scan (ThriftVision)
*   Tap the **Camera Icon** on the navbar.
*   Point your phone at a single book spine or cover.
*   **The Magic:** AI identifies the title/author and immediately gives you a **BUY** or **PASS** recommendation based on real-time market data and your personal profit thresholds.

### Step 2: Bulk Digitizing (ShelfVision) - *Pro Feature*
*   Facing a wall of 500 books? Switch to **Batch Mode**.
*   Take one high-resolution photo of the entire shelf.
*   **The Magic:** AI "reads" every spine in the photo simultaneously, adding them to your scan history and flagging the high-value items instantly.

---

## 📈 3. Phase Two: Strategic Analysis (Vision Intelligence)
You’ve bought the books. Now, let’s optimize your profit.

### Step 3: Series Tracking (SetVision)
*   Open a scanned book in your **Library**.
*   If it's part of a series (like *The Lord of the Rings*), tap **Analyze Set**.
*   **The Magic:** AI tells you exactly which volumes you are missing and calculates the "Set Completion Bonus"—the extra profit you'll make if you sell the full set instead of individual books.

### Step 4: Trend Forecasting (ForecastVision) - *Enterprise Feature*
*   Tap **Market Forecast** on a high-value item.
*   **The Magic:** AI predicts demand for the next 6 months. It might tell you: *"Hold this textbook until August; demand increases by 40% during Back-to-School season."*

---

## 📦 4. Phase Three: The Sale (Listing & Logistics)
Time to turn paper into paper (cash).

### Step 5: Auto-Drafting (ListingVision)
*   Tap **Generate Listing** on any book in your inventory.
*   Select your platform (eBay, Amazon, etc.).
*   **The Magic:** AI writes a professional, SEO-optimized title and description, selects the best keywords, and suggests a competitive price.

### Step 6: FBA Shipments (AmazonVision)
*   Ready to send a box to Amazon? Use the **Box Builder**.
*   Scan items as you pack them. The app tracks the total weight and ensures your shipment matches your digital manifest perfectly.

---

## 💰 5. Phase Four: The Financials (ProfitVision)
Keep every cent you earn.

### Step 7: Receipt Scanning (LedgerVision)
*   Finished at the store? Tap **Scan Receipt** in the Profit tab.
*   Take a photo of your paper receipt.
*   **The Magic:** AI extracts the total spent, tax, and item count, automatically logging your **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)** for tax season.

### Step 8: Mileage Tracking
*   Start the **GPS Tracker** when you leave your house.
*   **The Magic:** Every mile driven for sourcing is logged as a tax deduction, maximizing your end-of-year returns.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Scaling
1.  **Watch the Hub:** Check the **VisionHub** daily. It’s a curated feed of what *other* successful resellers are finding right now.
